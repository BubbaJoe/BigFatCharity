About Us

Big Fat Charity strives to provide the most up to date and transparent information possible in order to evaluate over 8,000 charities. We guide donors towards intelligent giving by providing them access to basic data about each charity. After analyzing thousands of documents, we have created a rating system to examine each charity’s performance in terms of financial health, accountability, and transparency. The ratings show givers how well we believe a charity will use their support and donations, how well it has maintained its programs and services over time, and how open they are with information. In doing so, donors are more confident in their giving.

Our team is comprised of five college students. Joe Williams is a computer science major from East Carolina University and worked mainly on the back end of the website. Aghil Zadeh is a graduate student at Duke University currently working on his PhD in Physics and was able to gather, organize, and analyze the data for the website.
